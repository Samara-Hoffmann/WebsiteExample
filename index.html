ABOUT US

Welcome to Taste of India, a place where the wonderful tastes of India come together in a friendly and cozy setting. Our story began with a love for the incredible flavors found in Indian cuisine.Inspired by family recipes and a deep appreciation for cooking, Taste of India was created as a way to share the joy of Indian flavors.


Our restaurant, started by the Gulati family in 1952, brings together generations of culinary traditions. Our family's roots are linked to the lively kitchens of North India, where the magic of mixing spices and making unforgettable dishes has been passed on through time. Today, we're thrilled to share those same cherished recipes and time-tested methods with you.


Quality is our promise at Taste of India. We take great care in selecting the best ingredients, often from local farms and partners who share our commitment to being kind to the environment. Every bite you take, from the first to the last, is a testament to the difference that fresh, carefully chosen ingredients can make.


Our connection to the community runs deep, and we believe in giving back. We proudly support local causes, events, and projects that bring positive change to the lives of those around us. When you dine with us, you're not just enjoying a meal, but you're joining us in making our community better.


We warmly invite you to experience the journey of flavors, stories, and friendships that Taste of India offers. Discover the heart and soul of India through our lovingly prepared dishes. Each plate is a prepared with love, and served with a smile. From our family to yours, welcome to Taste of India.
